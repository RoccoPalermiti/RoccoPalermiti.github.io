We have Any space
About

WehaveAnyspace is a real estate company located in the Netherlands. Anyforsoft helped realize their vision of a portal with a variety of real estate solutions for businesses worldwide. WehaveAnyspace serves as an intermediary helping companies find perfect office space rentals.

Implementation

Designed the web architecture to keep 'Content-to-Commerce' at the heart of its business model and let they fully operate on the motto of delivering best client experience. Custom module build-outs and integrations were implemented to accommodate the internal business flows. A custom library was integrated to allow the easier creation of many functions that were built for improved management.

Technology Stack

Language: PHP, SQL, HTML, CSS, Javascript
Framework: Laravel, Vue, Bootstrap, jQuery
Database: MySQL
Payment Gatway: Stripe
Server: AWS
Others: Social network, Email transfer
